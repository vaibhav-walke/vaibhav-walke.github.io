01 · About

Cloud-native by trade, pragmatic by habit.

I help enterprises run regulated, cloud-native workloads safely — designing the platforms, guardrails and developer experience that let engineering teams ship quickly without trading away security or reliability.

At Storebrand I led the AKS platform serving 500+ applications as Senior Cloud Architect. Since December 2025, as Senior Platform Engineer, I'm building a controlled, secured developer sandbox on Google Cloud Workstations for both core developers and citizen developers — so every team gets a reproducible, policy-bound environment for cloud-native and AI work.

I work across AWS, Azure and GCP, with deep focus on Kubernetes, Terraform, GitOps and IAM. I deploy LLM-powered applications on AKS and bring AI-assisted workflows into platform engineering — to speed up onboarding, reviews and day-to-day developer toil.

I also run internal Kubernetes trainings to help developers refactor and migrate their apps with confidence. Outside work: spicy Indian cooking and a good book.
